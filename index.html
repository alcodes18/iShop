Welcome to iShop.

A revolutionary chat based shop plugin! Featuring a clean smooth user interface, and loaded with tons of planned updates and development, whats not to love? Download iShop today, to start a cleaner, amazing economy experience on your Bukkit Server!

Please remember, without Vault installed, as well as a Economy plugin
this plugin will break, and crash your server!

If you're using any supported Economy Plugin with Vault integration, you are good to go!
